Eco-Friendly Living Guide - Site Plan

Site Name

Eco-Friendly Living Guide

This name was selected because it clearly communicates the website's purpose - to guide visitors toward more sustainable living practices. It's straightforward, memorable, and immediately conveys the site's environmental focus.

Optional domain availability: ecofriendlyliving.guide or greenlivinghub.com

Site Purpose

The Eco-Friendly Living Guide will serve as a comprehensive resource for sustainable living by:

  • Providing practical tips for reducing waste and conserving resources
  • Educating visitors about sustainable practices in various aspects of daily life
  • Offering interactive tools like a carbon footprint calculator
  • Creating a community space for eco-conscious individuals to connect
  • Showcasing sustainable products and alternatives to conventional items

Scenarios

These are typical questions that target audience members might ask:

  • How can I reduce my plastic waste in the kitchen?
  • What are the most energy-efficient appliances for my home?
  • Where can I find ethical clothing brands in my area?
  • What simple changes can my family make to live more sustainably?
  • How do I calculate and reduce my carbon footprint?

Color Schema

The selected color palette is inspired by nature and sustainability:

Primary Green (#2e8b57)

Used for headers, primary buttons, and important highlights

Secondary Green (#3cb371)

Used for accents, secondary buttons, and interactive elements

Light Green (#98fb98)

Used for backgrounds, highlights, and subtle accents

Dark Green (#006400)

Used for text headings and important information

Neutral Beige (#f5f5dc)

Used as the main background color for the site

Typography

The selected fonts provide excellent readability while maintaining an eco-friendly aesthetic:

Montserrat (Bold 700)

Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3, etc.) to create strong visual hierarchy and modern feel.

Roboto (Regular 400, Medium 500)

Used for body text, paragraphs, and navigation items for optimal readability across all devices.
